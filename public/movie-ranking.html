The Best Movies According to Christopher

My top 3 movies + a series of all-time.

The Shack

The most heart-healing movie ive ever seen, where it shows what it would be like to share a conversation with God would be like, and teaches a very meaningful lesson on forgiveness.

The Chosen

An excellent way to tell the Gospel of Jesus Christ in a surprisingly enjoyable way. Where the events are told through the unique perspectives of the 12 Disciples of Christ.

Ready Player One

A thrilling movie showing what one could do in Virtual Reality. Legit i would try it

Avatar

A stunningly beautiful sci-fi film where the main character fights on the side of very tall blue aliens to protect their land.
